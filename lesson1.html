🌐 Staying Safe Online

Let’s learn some easy tips to stay safe while using the internet!

  • 🔒 Never share your full name, address, or school online — keep it private!
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Always ask a parent or teacher before downloading a game or opening a link.
  • 🚫 If a message or picture makes you feel uncomfortable, tell an adult right away.
  • 💬 Be kind and respectful when chatting or commenting online.
  • 🎮 Only play age-appropriate games — check for “+7” or “+12” signs.
  • 📱 Remember: what you post online stays forever — so always think before posting.
  • 💡 When unsure, STOP, THINK, ASK!
