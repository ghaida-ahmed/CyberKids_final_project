Staying Safe Online

Learn simple habits that help you stay safe while using the internet.

  • Never share your full name, address, or school online. Keep it private.
  • Always ask a parent or teacher before downloading a game or opening a link.
  • If a message or picture makes you feel uncomfortable, tell an adult right away.
  • Be kind and respectful when chatting or commenting online.
  • Only play age-appropriate games. Check for age signs before starting.
  • Remember: what you post online stays forever, so always think before posting.
  • When unsure, stop, think, and ask.

Great job. You're learning how to be a smart and safe cyber kid.
